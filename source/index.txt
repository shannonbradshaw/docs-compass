What is |compass-short|?
------------------------

|compass| is the :abbr:`GUI (Graphical User Interface)` for MongoDB. Use Compass to read, write, and manage data in MongoDB Clusters.

.. _compass-and-community-eds:

|compass-short| Editions
------------------------

|compass-short| is available in four editions:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 25 70 5

   * - Edition
     - Description
     - Free

   * - Compass
     - Full-featured version of MongoDB Compass.
     - 

   * - Compass Community
     - Includes features than enable reading and writing data. 
     - :icon:`check-circle`

   * - Compass Readonly
     - For read-only access to data.
     - 

   * - Compass Isolated
     - Includes all features except those that require a network connection to services other than MongoDB.
     -
